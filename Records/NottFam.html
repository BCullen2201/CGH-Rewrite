These tables are by no means complete. They are the result of ongoing research in the indicated areas and are being posted as they become available. The first pass through will be to integrate my main set of notes on these families with another set of notebooks with further details, not yet connected in full. Later, as burial, christening, marriage, and other records and notes are fully connected, they will be included here as well.
