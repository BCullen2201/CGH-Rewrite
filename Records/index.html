The guidelines for the content of the material found in the Record Archives has been changed somewhat. The Record Archives is meant to include non-specific items such as census reports, immigration records, and other 'lists'. Specific family histories normally fall under 'Related Families' in the Family Trees pages. There is now a category for Cullen Family trees that, although not known to be related, may prove to be useful to other Cullen branches . Feel free to forward any material you believe to be useful but remember to check the copyright laws protecting the sources of your material.
