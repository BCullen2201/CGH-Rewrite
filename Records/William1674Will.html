Will of William Taylor
Yeoman of Oxton: 1674

Jim Cullen

William Taylor, Yeoman of Oxton, married Susanna Cullen, the daughter of Gervase & Katherine Cullen, Oct 12, 1671, at Halam, Nottinghamshire. Susanna at that time was the daugher of "Katherine, widow of Upton", since Susanna's father, Gervase Cullen, had died in 1665/1666 in Upton. William and Susanna were not married for a very long time and only had one son. The will is interesting in that the bulk of the estate was left to his wife Susanna and her siblings: Thomas Cullen, Jervas Cullen, Mary Cullen, Deborah Cullen, Katherine Cullen, and Sarah Cullen. William Taylor, Yeoman of Oxton, married Katherine's daughter Susanna. Interestingly, Richard Chapman, also of Oxton, married another of Katherine's daughters, Deborah, on Jun 30, 1677. This will has taught us that even the wills of close relatives of the Cullens can contain some very useful information for our research into Cullen family history.
