Cullen Will Transcriptions - Upton, Nottinghamshire

This page is in progress and is an effort to reorganize access to will transcriptions for Cullens in Upton, Nottinghamshire, and surrounding areas. I'm most interested in those wills of Cullen Family members descended from Richard Cullen of Upton, and whos wills were probated prior to about 1800. If anyone has relevant will transcriptions that they'd like to contribute, please contact us and we can have a page put up for your information fairly quickly. I will also be including Administration Bonds here when a will was not written. Perhaps these bonds are not as informative as wills but, as the available information begins to thin out, every little bit helps.

Wills: Cullen family members and close relatives, prior to about 1800
  • Richard Cullen (1580): Husbandman of Upton c.1540-1580. Long recognized as the earliest member of the Cullen Family in Upton, Nottinghamshire.
  • Margaret Robinson (1591): Spinster late of Upton. Had a brother Robert Cullen who was a contemporarie of Richard Cullen (c.1537-1580).
  • Thomas Cullen (1628): Yeoman and Churchwarden of Upton from 1600-1625. Widely believed to be the son of Richard Cullen above.
  • Susanna Cullen (1652): The wife of Thomas Cullen, Yeoman of Upton (c1573-1628). They were married in November of 1611 in Upton. There's lots of family detail in this will.
Administration Bonds
  • Thomas Cullin (1661): An unknown Thomas Cullen. His widow's name was Anne.
